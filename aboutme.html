About SmartTools

Welcome to SmartTools — your all-in-one online calculator and converter hub! Our goal is to make daily calculations and conversions fast, easy, and accessible to everyone — anytime, anywhere.

Whether you’re calculating your BMI, converting currency, checking your EMI, or figuring out your exact age in years, months, days, and minutes — SmartTools does it all instantly and accurately.

SmartTools was built and designed by Ayan (Abdul Rahim) — a passionate web developer from West Bengal, India 🇮🇳 with a love for clean UI, smooth animations, and simple tools that make life easier for everyone.

💡 SmartTools focuses on real-world usability — no ads, no distractions, just productivity. New tools are added regularly, so keep checking back for updates!

🌐 Our Mission

To provide accurate, free, and instant tools that help students, professionals, and everyday users complete their tasks with ease.

🔒 Transparency

We believe in user trust. SmartTools doesn’t collect personal information — all calculations happen locally in your browser. Ads (if any) are used only to support the development and hosting of this project.
